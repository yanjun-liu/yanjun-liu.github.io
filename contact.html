Contact

Questions?

Have a question, comment, complaint, or idea?

If you can't make it to a VFA event to talk to us in person please feel free to email us at (staff at vegasfur dot com). This email will go to all staff with an email address. To contact a certain member please check the About page for individual contacts.

Chat

For chat about planning and events strictly please go to #VegasFur on FurNet. There you can chat with the Bunny Man and possibly others. Eventually we make this more accessible with a on page chat.

Local Furry Communities & Businesses

If you want to meet other local furries online we personally recommend and endorse Las Vegas Furs Community. There is also several different Facebook and meet up groups. Check the blogroll along the bottom of every page for links to lots of local communities, fur friendly companies, and other related links.
