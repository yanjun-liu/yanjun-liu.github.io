Fursuiting in Las Vegas

Tue 11 September 2012
By tBunnyMan

In Editorial.

I finally got around to knocking another big task off my list of things to do and sat down to do all the research and post up a full page of information on fursuiting in Las Vegas! Check along the top bar for the link to the new suiting research page!

It's not complete by a long shot yet and it really needs your help. If you have fursuited publicly or semipublicly in the last year I really need your feedback on different locations, problems you have encountered, and awesome business owners that were cool with you suiting.

Please leave comments in the post or send me an email so I can grow this into a great resource for all local suitors and suiters to be!

One thing I want the page to highlight, at least initially, is that we need to be aware of some of the laws being written and passed right now concerning public performance. When fur suiting we are put into the same category as all other street performers in town and are subject to our laws. Earlier this year most of those laws were smashed because of some amazing court cases defending our first amendment rights but Oscar Goodman and others have made it clear they want to heavily restrict the locations in which you can suit and possibly even require a permit on a per person basis.

P.S. Don't forget the con naming contest is still going on!
